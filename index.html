About Us

Sassafras Nuts is a commercial chestnut and walnut farm located at Sassafras in the Budawang Ranges, 50 km south west of Nowra N.S.W. We have been growing premium quality nuts for more than 25 years.

Our farm gate is just two hours and forty minutes drive from Sydney; ninety minutes from Wollongong; two hours from Canberra and forty minutes from Nowra.  We welcome visitors and groups to the farm during the autumn harvest to purchase nuts.

All nuts picked by you are charged per kilogram.  If you prefer, we can supply you with nuts already picked and graded for a slightly higher price per kilogram depending on grade and variety.

Buckets and bags for picking and packing are supplied for your convenience.

If you require walnuts these can be purchased directly from our packing shed when available.

For large orders of pre-packed chestnuts in 10 kilogram bags please telephone to make an order.

Please note that no pets are permitted on the property.

WHAT TO BRING

SUGGESTED CLOTHING AND GLOVES

Sassafras is located at an elevation of 750 metres and in autumn can be cooler and wetter than the surrounding Illawarra region.

It is advisable to bring weather proof coats and shoes as well as gloves for picking chestnuts.

What are you doing about COVID 19 in relation to your business?

> We have a COVID 19 policy in the interest of the health and well being of all our customers and staff. For details link to Sassafras Nuts COVID 19 Policy. Do not visit the farm if you have symptoms or have just returned from overseas (within the self isolation period). Bring your own hand wipes and hand sanitiser if visiting. Observe social distancing whilst visiting the farm and after that have a great time.
If you are concerned about making a visit this season, please call us and we will discuss options such as pre-order, drive through, mail service and delivery depending on the volume of your orders
